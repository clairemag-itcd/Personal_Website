WHAT AM I AFRAID OF?


Achluophobia

I really can't sleep without any amount of light. As i grow older my fear in darkness develop. It caused me a lot of stress whenever i feel alone in the dark. I also avoid watching horror movies because i know later on it will affect the way i sleep. I know myself that i should be avoiding things that can cause stress on me. It's better for me to avoid things that is clearly aren't for me.



Sociophobia

My fear with people started when a classmate from my junior high school bullied for no reason. Everytime i stand up infront of the class he always laughs at me. From that moment i started to loose my confidence. Also, everytime there is a family gathering, there is always their favorite child among the siblings, and i am always the one who is left out.
