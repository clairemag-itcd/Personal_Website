ABOUT ME.

My name is Claire Anne S. Magalong, and I want to be a UI/UX Designer. I am currently enrolled in the University of the East, where I am studying Information Technology. I enjoyed spending time alone because it allowed me to have a better understanding of myself and the world around me.
